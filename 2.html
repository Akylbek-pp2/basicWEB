A little bit about Salam Bro

"Salam Bro" is a colloquial expression used among friends, especially in Muslim communities, to greet each other with warmth and respect. "Salam" comes from the Arabic word "السلام" (As-Salam), meaning "peace," and is part of the common greeting "As-Salamu Alaikum," meaning "peace be upon you." "Bro" is a casual, shortened form of "brother."
